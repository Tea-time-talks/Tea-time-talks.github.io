Tea time talks

Who are we?

Tea time talks is a group for the PhD students in the department of Animal Sciences and Aquaculture at the Norwegian University of Life Sciences. We meet every week to discuss a paper, a book, or a topic of interest. The goal is to learn from each other and to have fun.

Categories of the talks

How to join?

If you are a PhD student in the department of Animal Sciences and Aquaculture at the University of Norwegian University of Life Sciences, you are welcome to join us. Just send an email to the organizer of the week and you will be added to the mailing list.
